01 Overview

Equity Solutions Network ("we," "our," or "us") operates equitysolutionsnetwork.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site or contact us about our services.

We take your privacy seriously. We are not in the business of selling personal data — we're in the business of solving problems. This policy is written in plain language on purpose.

The short version: We use your contact information only to respond to your inquiry and, if you opt in, to send you occasional updates. We don't sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose you didn't agree to.

02 Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to respond to your inquiry and operate the site:

  • Name and email address — submitted via our contact form.
  • Organization name — optionally provided in the contact form.
  • Service interest — the service area you select when contacting us.
  • Message content — what you write in the contact form.
  • Email marketing opt-in status — whether you checked the box to receive updates.
  • Usage data — anonymized analytics (page views, session duration) via Google Analytics.
  • Browser/device data — standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) retained briefly for security.
  • Market signal data — anonymized, aggregate usage data from our MyJobDaddy platform is used to power market insights on ESN. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

03 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for these purposes only:

  • Responding to your contact form submission.
  • Following up on service inquiries.
  • Sending updates and insights if you opted in (see Section 04).
  • Improving overall site performance using aggregated, anonymized analytics.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your contact details are never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for commercial purposes.

04 Email Marketing

If you checked the opt-in box on our contact form, you agreed to receive occasional email updates from Equity Solutions Network. These may include insights on AI governance, IT strategy, platform updates, and ESN service announcements.

We send a low volume of emails — we will never spam you. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time with a single click, no questions asked.

If you did not check the opt-in box, you will only receive a reply to your specific inquiry and nothing further.

To unsubscribe at any time: Click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email from us, or email info@equitysolutionsnetwork.com with the subject "Unsubscribe" and we will remove you within 2 business days.

05 Data Storage & Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 2 years to allow us to maintain a record of our communications and respond to follow-up inquiries.

If you opted into email marketing, your email address is retained in our marketing list until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Server logs (IP addresses, access times) are retained for up to 90 days for security monitoring, then automatically deleted.

06 Third Parties

We work with a small number of trusted service providers to operate the site:

  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — Email delivery for contact form notifications and marketing emails. Subject to Brevo's own privacy policy.
  • Railway — Hosting and infrastructure. Covered by their data processing agreements.
  • Cloudflare — DNS and performance. Processes traffic metadata per their privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics — Anonymized usage analytics. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google.
  • Anthropic — AI processing powering our MyJobDaddy platform. Market signal data passed to ESN is fully anonymized and aggregated.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes.

07 Cookies & Analytics

We use a small number of cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Session cookies — Necessary for basic site functionality. Deleted when you close your browser.
  • Google Analytics (gtag.js) — Collects anonymized data about how visitors use the site (pages visited, session duration). This data does not include personally identifiable information.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

08 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your contact records and/or email marketing subscription.
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@equitysolutionsnetwork.com. We will respond within 30 days.

09 Requesting Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you:

  • Remove from email marketing list — Click the unsubscribe link in any email, or email us with subject "Unsubscribe." Removed within 2 business days.
  • Delete all contact data — Email us with subject "Delete All My Data" and the email address you used. We will permanently delete your contact records within 5 business days and confirm by reply.

Contact for all data requests:
Email: info@equitysolutionsnetwork.com
Subject: "Delete All My Data" or "Unsubscribe"
Include: the email address you used when contacting us
Response time: within 5 business days

10 Children's Privacy

Equity Solutions Network is intended for business professionals and organizations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our site, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11 Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we'll provide notice on our homepage or via email if we have your address on file.

Continued use of equitysolutionsnetwork.com after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
